Legal & Privacy

How this portal handles your data, what cookies are used for, and the terms of using your cohort access.

🔒

Privacy Policy

This portal collects the information needed to manage your cohort access: your name, email, cohort group, and basic progress data (modules completed, workbooks submitted). This information is used to track your progress and personalize your dashboard. It is never sold, and is not shared outside the program except where required to operate the portal itself.

You can request a copy of the data tied to your account, or request it be deleted, by contacting support.

🍪

Cookie Policy

This portal uses essential cookies to keep you logged in and remember your cohort progress. It does not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Disabling cookies may prevent the portal from functioning correctly, including staying logged in.

📄

Terms of Use

Member access is personal to the enrolled participant and should not be shared. Course material (video, workbooks, templates) is provided for the member's own use during the program and is not licensed for redistribution, resale, or public posting without permission.

Access may be suspended or removed for misuse, including sharing login credentials or redistributing course material outside the cohort.

This page is a placeholder summary for planning purposes. Final legal language should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before this portal goes live.
