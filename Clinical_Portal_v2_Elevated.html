Compliance & Data Practices

How this portal handles data, and what this library is, and is not, intended for.

🔒

HIPAA & Patient Privacy

This portal does not store, display, or process patient-identifiable health information. All case studies in the Research Library and Protocol Vault are de-identified before publication, consistent with HIPAA's de-identification standard. Member accounts are tied to the clinician or practice, not to any patient, and no patient record is ever uploaded, searchable, or retained within this system.

Clinicians remain solely responsible for HIPAA compliance within their own practice and patient records, which are managed entirely outside this portal.

⚖

FDA & Regulatory Notices

Material in this library is provided for clinical education and peer reference only. Nothing here constitutes a treatment recommendation, a claim of safety or effectiveness, or marketing for any specific product or procedure. Where source material discusses regenerative or biologic therapies, that material reflects the cited research and is not an endorsement of off-label or unapproved use.

Clinicians are responsible for independently verifying current FDA status, applicable indications, and regulatory requirements for any product or protocol referenced in this library before clinical use.

📋

Data Handling & Security

Member accounts are protected by individual login credentials. Activity within the portal (logins, items viewed, downloads) is logged for account security and usage reporting, not shared externally, and not linked to any patient data.

This page is a placeholder summary for planning purposes. Final compliance language should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before this portal goes live.
